Quick Start Guide

Converting dates between Ethiopian and Gregorian calendars is simple:

  1. Select your input calendar (Ethiopian or Gregorian)
  2. Enter the date you want to convert and wait the magic to happen

Example: To convert Ethiopian date "1 Meskerem 2015"

  • Enter day: 1
  • Select month: Meskerem
  • Enter year: 2015

Understanding Ethiopian Calendar

The Ethiopian calendar:

  • Has 13 months (12 months of 30 days each, plus Pagume)
  • Pagume has 5 days (6 days in leap years)
  • Is approximately 7-8 years behind the Gregorian calendar
Tip: Ethiopian New Year typically falls on September 11th or 12th (Gregorian)
